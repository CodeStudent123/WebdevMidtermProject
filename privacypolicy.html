Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Summary

We do not share or sell your personal information. We are strongly committed to protecting user privacy and being as transparent as possible.

We collect only the information necessary for our products and services to function properly.

The Weekly Journal apps filter traffic locally on your device. We don’t know what websites you visit.

The Weekly Journal apps periodically connect to our servers to download filter updates and check your license status.

You are not required to share your personal data with us. You can delete your information by deleting your The Weekly Journal account at my.theweeklyjournal.com or by sending us a request at privacy@theweeklyjournal.com.

Who We Are

How We Collect Personal Data

There are several ways we may collect your Personal Data:

  • When you visit our website theweeklyjournal.com and its subdomains.

What Data We Process

We process only the data necessary for the functionality of The Weekly Journal products and services.

We do not process data for tracking purposes and take all necessary measures to protect the information we collect.

Account Registration

We process your email address and a hash of your password for registration purposes.

Sending Messages

We may use your email address to send announcements, updates, and notifications.

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.

Cookies

We use cookies to personalize website content, such as setting the default language.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some services may not function properly.

Buying a License

If you purchase a license, we will use your email address to manage your purchase and account.

Payments are processed via Paddle.com Market Ltd. and PayPro Global, Inc.

For details on data collected by payment providers, refer to Paddle’s Privacy Policy and PayPro Global’s Privacy Policy.
